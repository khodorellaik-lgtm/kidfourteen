Bio

Kid Fourteen is the musical alias of Khodor Ellaik — a Lebanese musician, composer, and sound artist based in Paris. His work blends experimental pop, noise, and emotional songwriting, bridging tenderness and rupture. Over the years, he has released multiple albums exploring love, trauma, and absolution.

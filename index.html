Bio

Short version.

Kid Fourteen is the project of Paris-based musician and sound artist Khodor Ellaik, blending synth-pop, noise, and saxophone-driven experimentation into confessional songs and volatile live sets. Previous work includes collaborations with artists across the experimental pop and noise spectrum.

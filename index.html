Obscure Sound
“Khodor Ellaik, aka Kid Fourteen, is a Paris-based Lebanese musician and songwriter who has been intriguing listeners for over a decade with his unique style of mixing Pop, Post-Punk, Noise, experimental Jazz and Electronic Music. The 12-track project is the artist’s personal declaration of survival and autobiography, in all its abstraction.”

— Obscure Sound

Obscure Sound
